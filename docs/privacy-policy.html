Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

RetailAgent ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • API Keys: We store your Gemini API key locally in Chrome's local storage. This key is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Shopping Preferences: Your preferred platforms, payment methods, and addresses are stored locally.
  • Order Information: Order details are stored locally for tracking purposes.

2.2 Information We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process your shopping intents and queries
  • Store your preferences and settings
  • Track your orders (stored locally)
  • Improve the extension's functionality

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API. We do not maintain any servers or databases that store your information.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Gemini API

We use Google's Gemini API to process natural language queries. Your API key and queries are sent directly to Google's servers. Please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5.2 Google Maps API (Optional)

If you enable store locator features, we may use Google Maps API. Location data is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Permissions

RetailAgent requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To interact with ecommerce websites
  • storage: To save your preferences and settings locally
  • tabs: To open ecommerce platforms in new tabs
  • scripting: To inject content scripts for automation
  • geolocation (optional): For store locator features

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through Chrome's extension storage
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Modify your preferences at any time through settings

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • API keys are encrypted in Chrome's secure storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers (except API calls you initiate)

9. Children's Privacy

RetailAgent is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@retailagent.com
  • GitHub: Open an issue

12. Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
